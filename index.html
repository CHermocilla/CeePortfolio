About Me

Hey! I'm Ceeline, a fourth-year IT student at PHINMA St. Jude College of Manila. Tech has always been my thing - I was the kid who just got computers, and that curiosity never really went away.

I started with C++ and Java, fell into web development through my capstone project, and somehow ended up making a medieval fighting game with MonoGame. Five projects later, I'm still finding new things to build.

In group projects I tend to take the lead and get hands-on with everything. I'd rather figure something out by doing it than just read about it.

Long term? I want to be deep in the tech industry - and somewhere down the line, ship an indie game of my own.
